Welcome to Digital Forensics Using Blockchain

Cybercrime investigations are becoming more challenging due to advanced techniques used by criminals to hide illicit activities. Traditional forensic tools help recover deleted files but cannot guarantee the security and authenticity of evidence. Our project uses blockchain technology to create a secure and unchangeable record of digital evidence. This ensures that forensic data remains protected, traceable, and legally valid, making investigations more reliable and transparent.

About Our Project

Digital forensics is essential for solving cybercrimes, but existing methods often fail to ensure evidence integrity. Criminals use techniques like steganography to hide data, making detection difficult. Our system combines forensic analysis with blockchain security to securely recover, analyze, and store evidence. By maintaining a tamper-proof record, it helps investigators preserve critical data and strengthens legal proceedings against cybercrimes.
